# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Agent Dance is for people watching foreign-language talks, courses, conferences, and recorded meetings. They need to understand the content while it is happening, not after a long post-processing step. Primary users include students, developers, knowledge workers, and enterprise viewers who rely on clear Chinese subtitles.

## Product Purpose

The product turns a one-way foreign-language audio or video stream into Chinese subtitles in real time or after upload. Success means users can follow the speaker's pace, see readable Chinese subtitles, and understand when an earlier subtitle was corrected.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, dependable. The interface should feel like a serious translation console, not a marketing page or a lightweight prototype.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI-purple gradients, hard black dashboards, decorative glassmorphism, oversized hero copy, long landing pages, fake enterprise buzzwords, and UI that hides the actual interpretation workflow behind marketing sections.

## Design Principles

- Put the live interpretation workflow first.
- Make status changes visible in plain language.
- Use visual polish to support trust, not decoration.
- Keep the interface compact enough to fit in one viewport.
- Preserve a clear path to backend integration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for body text, controls, badges, and subtitle content. Motion should be short, state-driven, and covered by reduced-motion fallbacks. Chinese subtitles must remain readable at desktop and mobile sizes.
